Organisation Overview

Name: Ntando Agriculture

Ntando Agriculture is a small-to-medium, Black-owned agribusiness based in Bloemfontein, South Africa. Founded in 2025 by Ntando Nonjola, the company focuses on sustainable crop farming, organic produce, and community empowerment. Ntando Agriculture operates across several hectares of land, cultivating fresh vegetables and fruit, which are sold to local markets and retailers.

Mission: To promote food security and healthy living by providing fresh, organic, and locally sourced produce through sustainable farming practices.

Vision: To become one of the leading agro-businesses in South Africa, inspiring the next generation of tech-savvy and environmentally conscious farmers.

Target Audience:

  • Local consumers
  • Retail stores
  • Restaurants
  • Schools and community feeding schemes
  • Agriculture investors and sponsors

Welcome to Ntando Agriculture

Where sustainable livestock farming meets community-driven agriculture. We specialize in ethical cattle herding, organic farming, and empowering local farmers to create a brighter, more sustainable future.

About Our Livestock Farming

At Ntando Agriculture, we raise cattle and livestock with the utmost care and attention, ensuring healthy, ethically bred animals that contribute to local food security. Our cattle are raised on sustainable farms that prioritize their well-being and the health of the environment.

Supporting Local Farmers

We collaborate with neighboring farmers to promote agricultural innovation and share resources, strengthening the farming community in Bloemfontein and beyond. Together, we cultivate crops, raise livestock, and nurture opportunities for growth.
