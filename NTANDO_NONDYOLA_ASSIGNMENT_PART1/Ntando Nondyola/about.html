About Us

Welcome to Ntando Agriculture, a proudly Black-owned agribusiness situated in Bloemfontein, South Africa. Established in 2025, we are passionate about sustainable farming and organic agriculture.

Our Mission

We aim to enhance food security and healthy living by providing high-quality, organic livestock and crops while promoting environmentally friendly farming practices.

Our Vision

To become one of South Africa's leading agribusinesses and inspire a new generation of tech-savvy, eco-conscious farmers.

Meet the Owners

Ntando Nonjola: The visionary behind Ntando Agriculture, Ntando Nonjola is a dedicated farmer and entrepreneur with a deep-rooted passion for sustainable agriculture. Ntando is committed to uplifting the community through innovative farming practices and creating opportunities for growth in the agricultural sector.

Ntando Nonjola leads the livestock and cattle farming operations with expertise and care, ensuring ethical animal rearing and quality produce for the local market.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: Prioritizing eco-friendly farming techniques.
  • Community Empowerment: Supporting local initiatives and growth.
  • Quality: Providing fresh, organic produce and ethically raised livestock.
